Synchronizing Files


Synchronizing Files on Demand

  1. In the status bar, right-click the iFolder application icon , then click My iFolders to open the My iFolders browser.

  2. In the list of iFolders, select the iFolder, then click Properties.

  3. Click Sync Now .


Configuring the Synchronization Interval

  1. In the status bar, right-click the iFolder application icon , then select Preferences to open the iFolder Preferences dialog box to the General tab.

  2. Do one of the following:

    • Select Sync to enable background synchronization.
    • Deselect Sync to disable synchronization.

  3. If you enable synchronization, specify the interval for synchronizing files, such as every 1 hour or every 2 days.

    The minimum Sync Interval, typically every 5 minutes, is configured by the iFolder administrator. You cannot override the system minimum.
