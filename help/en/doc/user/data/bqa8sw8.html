Deleting an Address Book

Deleting an address book from the Books list also deletes the contacts recorded in that book. You cannot delete the Default address book.

  1. In the Address Book, do one of the following:

    • Right-click the book in the Books list, then click Delete.
    • Select the Book, then click Edit > Delete.

    A confirmation dialog opens.

  2. Click Yes to continue with the deletion, or click No to cancel it.

    If Yes, the address book disappears from the Books list and all of the contacts that were in the deleted book are also deleted.

  3. Close Address Book.
