Setting Up an Available iFolder

When others share iFolders with you, the iFolders appear with an Available iFolder icon in your My iFolders browser. If you want to participate in the iFolder on your local computer, you must set up the iFolder to make it active on the computer.

  1. In the status bar, right-click the iFolder icon , then click My iFolders to open the My iFolders browser.

  2. In the list of iFolders, select the available iFolder, then click Set Up .

  3. Log in to the account in that domain using your username and password.

  4. Click OK.
