Deleting an iFolder

Deleting an iFolder has different results, depending on your membership status in the iFolder workgroup.

  • As a Bill owner or Admin owner of an iFolder, deleting an iFolder permanently removes the iFolder and its contents from the local workstation and from the workstations of all related member iFolders.
  • As a Read/Write user or Read Only user, deleting an iFolder removes the iFolder and its contents permanently from your local workstation only and cancels your share relationship.

Deleting an iFolder does not uninstall the iFolder client from your workstation.

  1. In a file manager or on the desktop, locate the iFolder that you want to delete.

  2. Right-click the iFolder, then click Delete.
