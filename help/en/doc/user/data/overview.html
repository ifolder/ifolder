Overview of iFolder

iFolderTM is a file-sharing application for Linux, Windows*, and Macintosh* clients. You can share files in multiple iFolders, each with a different group of users. You control who can participate in an iFolder and the access level for each member. You can also participate in iFolders that others share with you.
