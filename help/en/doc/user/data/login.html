Logging In to the iFolder Client

You can log in to the client using one of the following approaches:

  • Enable Remember Password upon account activation so that log in occurs automatically whenever you log in to your desktop.
  • Disable Remember Password and log in whenever you log in to your desktop.
  • Disable Remember Password and log in only when you are ready to synchronize files.

IMPORTANT:  If you do not save your password, the Login dialog box is available only when you log in to your desktop and when iFolder prompts you to log in for syncing.

You must be logged in to iFolder to enable background syncing. The synchronization frequency for files is governed by the Sync Interval for your iFolder account. There is also a 1-minute synchronization interval for refreshing the list of iFolders you are invited to participate in, but this is not exposed through the user interface.

Whenever one of the sync intervals times out, if you are not logged in at that time, iFolder prompts you to log in. You can log in to allow background syncing, or you can click Cancel to postpone syncing temporarily until a sync interval elapses again.

NOTE:  In the final release, iFolder will allow you to disable the periodic prompting behavior and access the Login dialog box on demand. The Sync Interval in iFolder Preferences will be used for syncing iFolder files and invitations. In addition, you will be able to refresh the iFolder invitations on demand.
