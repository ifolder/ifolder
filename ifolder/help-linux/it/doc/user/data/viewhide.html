3.7 Viewing and Hiding Available iFolders

You can decide which iFolders you want to download to each of your computers. If iFolders are available to you on the server that you have not downloaded, they are listed in the iFolder browser under the related account.

To view the available iFolders in the iFolder browser, click View Available iFolders.

To hide the available iFolders in the iFolder browser, click Hide Available iFolders.
